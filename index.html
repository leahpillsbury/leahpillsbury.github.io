Leah Pillsbury

Leah is a nonprofit fundraiser based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

She is currently the Capital Campaign Director for Farm & Wilderness , Farm & Wilderness a nonprofit, educational organization operating six summer camps and year-round educational programming. Previous positions include Director of Development for Common Market Philadelphia, and Capital Campaign Coordinator for Mariposa Food Co-op.

In her volunteer time Leah raises money for organizations and candidates she believes in, while also encouraging others to think more radically about the money they are giving away or investing.

She currently serves on the planning committee for Bread and Roses Community Fund's Annual Tribute to Change. She is a member of Resource Generation, a national nonprofit that organizes young people with financial wealth to leverage resources and privilege for social change. Leah is also a member of the Association of Fundraising Professionals.

To learn more about Leah you can read an essay she wrote for Grid Magazine on socially responsible investing or watch a short investor video profile of her produced by The Reinvestment Fund.

Perhaps most notably, Leah is the founder and curator at Hair's What We Do, a photo blog dedicated to showcasing #punny hair salons and barbershops from around the world; it is actively soliciting submissions.

She can be reached at info@leahpillsbury.com.
