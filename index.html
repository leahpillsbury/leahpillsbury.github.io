Leah Pillsbury

Leah is a nonprofit fundraiser based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

She currently works for Common Market Philadelphia, a nonprofit distributor of local foods, as Director of Development and Communication. Previously, she worked as a freelance fundraiser and is particularly proud of her success in leading the Mariposa Food Co-op Capital Campaign.

In her professional work and volunteer time Leah works with organizations that care for and progress her city and neighborhood. She continues to be involved with Mariposa Food Co-op and serves on the board of The Enterprise Center Community Development Corporation in West Philadelphia. She is an active member of the Friends of Saint Bernard Community Garden who are currently fighting to keep the garden land in community hands. Leah is also a member of the Association of Fundraising Professionals.

Perhaps most notably, Leah is the founder and curator at Hair's What We Do, a photo blog dedicated to showcasing #punny hair salons and barbershops from around the world; it is actively soliciting submissions.

She can be reached at info@leahpillsbury.com.
