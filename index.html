Leah Pillsbury

Leah is a nonprofit fundraiser based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

She currently works for Common Market Philadelphia, a nonprofit distributor of local foods, as Director of Development and Communication. Previously, she worked as a freelance fundraiser and is particularly proud of her success in leading the Mariposa Food Co-op Capital Campaign.

In her professional work and volunteer time Leah raises money for organizations she cares about, while also encouraging others to think more radically about the money they are giving away or investing.

She currently serves on the board of The Enterprise Center Community Development Corporation in West Philadelphia. She is a member of Resource Generation, a national organization that organizes young people with financial wealth to leverage resources and privilege for social change. Leah is also a member of the Association of Fundraising Professionals.

To learn more about Leah you can read a recent essay she wrote for Grid Magazine on socially responsible investing or watch a short investor video profile of her produced by The Reinvestment Fund.

Perhaps most notably, Leah is the founder and curator at Hair's What We Do, a photo blog dedicated to showcasing #punny hair salons and barbershops from around the world; it is actively soliciting submissions.

She can be reached at info@leahpillsbury.com.
