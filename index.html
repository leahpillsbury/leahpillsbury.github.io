Leah Pillsbury

Leah is a nonprofit fundraiser and apsiring accountant based in Philadelphia.

She is currently pursuing her Master of Accountancy at Temple University.

Most recently, she was the Capital Campaign Director for Farm & Wilderness, a nonprofit organization operating six summer camps in Vermont. She previously worked for Common Market as Director of Development. Before that, Leah managed Mariposa Food Co-op's capital campaign for its relocation and expansion.

In her volunteer time, Leah raises money for organizations and political candidates she believes in, while also encouraging others to think more radically about the money they are giving away or investing.

She serves on the Board of Directors of Bread and Roses Community Fund, a community of donors and activists that raises and distributes funds to support social justice work the Philadelphia region. She is a member of Resource Generation, a national nonprofit that organizes young people with financial wealth to leverage resources and privilege for social change.

Perhaps most notably, Leah is the founder and curator at Hair's What We Do, a photo blog (and accompanying Instagram) dedicated to showcasing punny hair salons and barbershops from around the world; it is actively soliciting submissions.
